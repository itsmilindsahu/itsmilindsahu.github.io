## What I worked on today

Today I started my research internship journey and created a system to document my daily progress. Setting up a structured approach to record my learning journey is the first step toward building a comprehensive research portfolio.

## Key learning

I learned how important it is to record research progress consistently, including:

- Papers I read
- Concepts I learned
- Experiments I tried
- Problems I faced
- Ideas for future work

Maintaining a daily journal helps me:

1. **Consolidate knowledge** - Writing forces me to articulate my understanding
2. **Track progress** - Looking back shows growth over time
3. **Identify gaps** - Regular reflection highlights what needs more work
4. **Prepare for discussions** - Having structured notes helps in mentor meetings

## Research notes

I want to use this section to record:

- Papers I read and my notes on them
- Concepts I learned and how they connect to my work
- Experiments I tried and their results
- Problems I faced and how I solved them
- Ideas for future work and research questions
- Weekly reflections on progress
- Questions to discuss with mentors
