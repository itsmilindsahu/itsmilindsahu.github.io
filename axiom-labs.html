01 What is AXL

Axiom Labs (AXL) is an independent computational research identity — a one-person research collective operating at the intersection of pure mathematics, algorithms, and applied systems.

The name reflects a core principle: build from axioms. Every system should have a rigorous theoretical foundation before implementation. No shortcuts, no duct tape.

AXL is the identity under which I compete in national hackathons, develop independent research projects, and publish technical work. It allows me to maintain a coherent research identity across institutions, competitions, and collaborators.

Currently focused on: energy systems analytics, optimization for policy design, and machine learning with mathematical guarantees.
