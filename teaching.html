03 Teaching Initiative

MB LEARNERS.

Peer Learning · IISER Tirupati

IISER courses can be brutal — especially interdisciplinary ones. MB Learners is a peer-learning group where we trade skills to survive the semester together.

We are not a coaching centre. We are a group of students who realized that "Math people need help with Biology, and Bio people need help with Math."

The initiative runs informal tutorial sessions, shares proof shortcuts, and breaks down mathematically heavy topics into approachable explanations.

"If you're at IISER Tirupati and struggling with a subject, there's always a seat here."
