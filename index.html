01 About

I'm a first-year BS-MS student at IISER Tirupati, working at the intersection of mathematical modeling, optimization theory, and computational methods for real-world systems.

My work spans energy analytics, machine learning, and policy analysis. I build systems that translate mathematical theory into deployable tools — from NILM-based energy disaggregation to proof-theoretic waste traceability frameworks.

Through my research identity AXL — Axiom Labs, I compete in national hackathons at IITs and IISERs, consistently placing in the top ranks against thousands of participants.

Goal: develop computational infrastructure for policy, infrastructure, and energy systems at scale.
